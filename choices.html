Your Privacy Choices

Complete transparency and control over your data with OnDeviceAI

🎉 Good News!

With OnDeviceAI, all your data stays on your device by default. You already have maximum privacy without needing to configure anything!

💬

Conversation Data

Your chat history is stored locally on your device and never transmitted anywhere. Here's how you can manage it:

Clear Individual Conversations

Status: Full Control ✓

  • Open OnDeviceAI app
  • Tap the trash icon during any conversation
  • Conversation is immediately deleted from your device

Clear All Data

Status: Complete Removal ✓

  • Go to iOS Settings → General → iPhone Storage
  • Find OnDeviceAI in the app list
  • Tap "Delete App" to remove all data permanently
🧠

AI Models

Downloaded AI models are stored locally and can be managed completely by you:

Delete Individual Models

Status: Full Control ✓

  • Open OnDeviceAI app
  • Navigate to Settings or Model Selector
  • Choose models to delete and free up storage

Choose What to Download

Status: Explicit Permission ✓

  • Models are never downloaded automatically
  • You choose which models to install
  • Cancel downloads anytime during the process
