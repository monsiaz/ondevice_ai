Frequently Asked Questions

How does the hybrid AI system work?

OnDeviceAI intelligently routes your queries between Apple Intelligence (for instant responses) and specialized MLX models (for advanced tasks). This gives you the best of both worlds: speed and sophistication.

What devices support Apple Intelligence?

Apple Intelligence requires iOS 18.1+ on iPhone 15 Pro or later, iPad with M1+, or Mac with M1+. On other devices, OnDeviceAI uses MLX models exclusively.

Do I need to download models to start using OnDeviceAI?

No! Apple Intelligence provides instant functionality with zero setup. MLX models are optional downloads for users who want enhanced capabilities for specific tasks.

How much storage do enhanced models require?

MLX models range from 350MB to several GB. Apple Intelligence requires no additional storage. The app recommends models based on your device's capabilities and available space.

Is my data really private?

Absolutely. OnDeviceAI has no servers, no analytics, no user accounts, and no data collection. Everything stays on your device, encrypted by iOS.
