Frequently Asked Questions

How is OnDeviceAI different from other AI assistants?

Unlike cloud-based AI services, OnDeviceAI runs entirely on your iPhone. Your conversations never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and allowing offline functionality.

What iPhone models are supported?

OnDeviceAI requires iOS 18.0+ and works best on devices with A12+ chips. Performance varies based on available RAM, with 6GB+ recommended for optimal experience.

Do I need internet connection?

Internet is only needed to download AI models initially. Once downloaded, OnDeviceAI works completely offline ‚Äî perfect for privacy-conscious users.

How much storage do the models require?

Models range from 350MB (Qwen 2.5 0.5B) to several GB for larger models. The app automatically recommends models based on your device's capabilities.

Is my data really private?

Absolutely. OnDeviceAI has no servers, no analytics, no user accounts, and no data collection. Everything stays on your device, encrypted by iOS.
